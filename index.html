In the 48 hours after Kobe Bryant's death was reported, he accumulated over 15 million pageviews on Wikipedia. The news of his death was reflected in the traffic.

More than 1700 notable people died in the last five years, according to Wikipedia. Here are 118 who got over half a million pageviews in the first 48 hours after their deaths.

Although no one grabbed our attention quite like Kobe Bryant, the spikes in pageviews after death a celebrity’s death can overshadow that of other major events, even a Grammy Awards won.

While not perfect, Wikipedia traffic serves as a solid proxy for the ebb and flow of a celebrity’s cultural relevance. It makes sense that household names like Kobe Bryantor Elizabeth II get the most traffic immediately following their death. The bigger the star (or tragedy), the more they are searched.
